Attractive popup forms on exit intent to convert visitors into subscribers.

== Description ==

Lead Capture is the best plugin to create beautiful popup forms on exit intent.
Stop loosing possible customers and start converting visitors into subscribers using one of our 4 attractive modal forms. 
The way this plugin works is when an user is about to leave your site, it shows a popup form to subscribe into your mailing list. This is perfect to grow your email list from all the visitors on your website that leaves before convert.
Now you can also integrate your MailChimp account and automatically add subscribers to your lists.

You can easily customize our 4 form templates with your content.

= Main Features =
* 4 Beautiful Templates
* Easy Customization
* MailChimp Support
* Install and start converting right away
* Export all your subscribers in a CSV file
* Customize the behavior of the popup form
* Free!

Lead Captor is the easiest way to grow your subscriber list.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `lead-captor.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Done

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I configure the site? =

Go to your **Admin Panel > Tools > Lead Captor**, and you will see all the settings.

= How do I export all the subscribers emails? =

Go to your **Admin Panel > Tools > Lead Captor > Export Subscribers**, and click on Export CSV to download a file with all the emails.
